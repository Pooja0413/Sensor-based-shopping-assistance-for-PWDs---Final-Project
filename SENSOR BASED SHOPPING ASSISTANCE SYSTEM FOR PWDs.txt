SENSOR BASED SHOPPING ASSISTANCE SYSTEM FOR PWDs


Steps involved in working(IOT):

Step 1: Install Blynk app.
step 2: Make sure the device is connected with the app.
Step 3: Scan the RFID card in the RFID reader.
Step 4: Ultrasonic sensor will detect the obstacles.



Output:

Notifications will be given on the following status:
•	RFID card scanning
•	Obstacle detection
